DESCRIPTION

Jollof Rice is african dish that is really sought after, As a matter of fact it's fought over by nearly all the powerhouses of Africa

It's origin is also a fight because, Ghana, Sengal as well as Nigeria claims they are the masters of it

Recently, One of Nigeria's chef and influencer took it upon herself partnering with Gino Nigeria to make the world's largest pot of Jollof.

Ingredients

  • 2cups of Rice
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 1/2 cup of tomato paste
  • 2 large fresh tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 2 cups of chicken stock
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash the rice in cold water until the water runs clear, then soak for 10 minutes and drain.
  2. Blend the fresh tomatoes, red bell pepper, scotch bonnet pepper and half of the onion until smooth.
  3. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the remaining onions and saut√© until translucent.
  4. Add the blended tomato mixture and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the tomato paste and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring frequently to prevent burning.
  6. Add the chicken stock, thyme, curry powder, bay leaf, and salt. Stir well to combine.
  7. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  8. Add the soaked and drained rice to the pot. Stir well to ensure the rice is evenly coated with the sauce.
  9. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and has absorbed all the liquid.
  10. Once cooked, fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious Jollof Rice!
